For High School Students: Are you looking for help with...


College Application Review?

If you are not tailoring certain aspects of your application to each school, your are not filling out the application correctly.

I will personally work with you as the student to tailor your essays, activities, and resume to the specific colleges and programs you are applying to, as well as help with interviews for selective schools.

College and Major Selection?

Did you know that some colleges have impacted majors while others don't?

I can help you to formulate a list of safety, target, and reach schools based on your passions whether they may be STEM/pre-med or pre-law/liberal arts. This can be especially helpful for you if you are an international student and many not be familiar with the nuances of American universities.

Course Planning?

A lot of students will try to take it easy senior year after chugging through AP/IB/honors courses freshman through junior year. This is a big mistake.

Regardless of whether you are a freshman, sophomore, or junior/soon-to-be-senior, I can help you with course planning to make sure you take a balanced but competitive workload based on the courses offered at your school.

For College Students: Are you interested in...


Product Management Roles?

I did three product management internships in college at Roblox, Google, and Johnson & Johnson and am intimantly familiar with the recruiting processes for APM programs/internships that have a < 2% acceptance rate.

I can help with reviewing resumes, interview prep, and coming up with projects that can help make you stand out from the crowd regardless of whether you are studying computer science, business, or even liberal arts.

Data Science/Quant Roles?

My internship at Johnson and Johnson was originally supposed to be a data science engineering internship and I did other internships in data science at Berkley startups in college and went through the interview processes/received offers from various hedge funds (ie. Jane Street, Citadel, Point72, etc.) for quant research internships.

Interviewing for data science/quant roles is incredibly challenging with multiple technical interviews related to your abilities in multivariable calculus, linear algebra, probability, and programming (depending on the position). I can help with interview and resume prep for these roles in both tech and finance.

Consulting Roles?

Getting a role as a summer or full time analyst in consultin is a question of being able to network properly and your ability to crack case interviews.

I can help guide you through this process having gone through processes for summer analyst positions at MBB firms. (Something cool to note is that if you are also interested in product management, frameworks for case interviews transfer over very nicely. Happy to chat more about this.)
