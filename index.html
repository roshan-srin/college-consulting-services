College: Are you interested in...


Product Management Roles?

I can help with reviewing resumes/LinkedIn, interview prep, and coming up with projects that can help make you stand out from the crowd regardless of whether you are studying computer science, business, or even liberal arts.

Data Science/Quant Roles?

High School: Do you need help with...


College Application Review?

If you are not tailoring certain aspects of your application to each school, you are not filling out the application correctly.

I will personally work with you as the student to tailor your essays, activities, and resume to the specific colleges and programs you are applying to, as well as help with interviews for selective schools.

College and Major Selection?

Did you know that some colleges have impacted majors while others don't?

I can help you to formulate a list of safety, target, and reach schools based on your passions whether they may be STEM/pre-med or pre-law/liberal arts. This can be especially helpful for you if you are an international student.

Course Planning?

A lot of students will try to take it easy senior year after chugging through AP/IB/honors courses freshman through junior year. This is a big mistake.

Regardless of whether you are a freshman, sophomore, or junior/soon-to-be-senior, I can help you with course planning to make sure you take a balanced but competitive workload based on the courses offered at your school.
